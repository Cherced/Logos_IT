Health and Research

Logos IT Networks has a strong ongoing connection to health care organisations and health research within Colombia.


Research and health care organisations have unique and specific data-storage software requirements based around the confidentiality, integrity and availability of information assets. We work with universities, health care providers and non-profit teams to build and configure what each project requires.


Our team is comprised of clinicians, researchers, data scientists and digital designers so we have a lot of lived experience working within clinical and research environments. We also know the complexities of working within grant- or project-based funding.
